## Move

Line (Atama|End) `Ctrl + A|E`

Word (Back|Forward) `Alt + B|F`

## Delete

Line (Undo|Korekara) `Ctrl + U|K`

Word (WorD) `Ctrl + W|D`

## Transpose (the last 2)

Characters `Ctrl + T`

Words `Esc + T`

## Format word

UPPERCASE `Esc + U`

lowercase `Esc + L`

Capitalize `Esc + C`

## Use background process

Move to background `Ctrl + Z`

List background `jobs`

Move to foreground `fg %#`

## List program

`apropos`
